Our Chapter


What is FBLA?
FBLA (Future Business Leaders of America) is an organization that helps to nurture future leaders, students, problem solvers, entrepreneurs and more. With almost a quarter million members, FBLA is growing in to be one of the biggest student led oragnizations. With many events to choose from, students have a large variety of selection from events ranging from complex tests to oral presentations.
